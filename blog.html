What makes video games so enticing and entertaining?

Published Date: 8/10/2023 Sources: 15 Reasons People Play Video Games and Esports Visual

Video games are a fantastic way to spend our free time, as they contain many pleasurable topics and escape reality. Video games are broken down into many factors, from gameplay, socializing, grinding, and exploration in an imaginary world that can take multiple forms. Games create a feeling of satisfaction when you spend a long time attempting to achieve something and then reward you for accomplishing the task. It can also vary in difficulty, transforming from easy-going and pleasant experiences to hellish and challenging tasks. The constant satisfaction from gaming creates an addiction, enticing the players to return for more and more each time they play.

The article 15 Reasons People Play Video Games by Cam Adair successfully lists numerous reasons people play video games. The reasons entail the novelty of a game. The many options to play with friends, complete quests, make alliances, obtain all items, and more classify the game as a novelty. Games can create a feeling of competition, motivating people to master said game, ultimately being the best. Through immersive experiences, people can explore worlds, take risks without suffering consequences, and have complete control of their progress and outcome. Video games can also be a source of income through esports and streaming.

FPS Shooters: what makes or breaks a game?

Published Date: 8/18/2023 Sources: Pixel Gun 3D and Team Fortress 2

Team Fortress 2

The factors that make a good game are content, replayability, frequent updates, balances, and free-to-play ability. FPS games come in various quality ranges, including the Call Of Duty franchise, Battlefield Franchise, Team Fortress, Overwatch, Valorant, and more. Some of the best games in the FPS genre are Call of Duty Cold War, Battlefield 1, and TF2. These games have hours of content to give players a massively multiplayer experience. During their prime, these games would receive frequent updates, fixing potential bugs and creating balances for fair gameplay. There are also no pay-to-win features, meaning the victor will only win based on skill.

Pixel Gun 3D

One of the worst games in this genre is Pixel Gun 3D. It was a humble, free mobile FPS game that allowed anybody to play. Eventually, the game turned for the worse, adding guns locked behind paywalls or weeks of playing for a single weapon, which could be easily purchased for a measly few bucks. They wouldn’t change this implementation and continued adding imbalance weapons, which took the skill out of the game. As a result, the game tragically died and lost most of its player base.

Toxicity: How to battle toxic players

Published Date: 9/12/2023 Sources: Esports: Toxic Players and Combating Toxicity

Toxicity in gaming is when players are rude and disrespectful to others by various means. Being toxic can be insulting others, making racial or rude remarks, teasing players who aren’t as skillful, and more. It’s essential to recognize toxicity since it can be a form of cyberbullying, depending on the severity, impacting victims' emotions. Those malicious remarks and behaviors can impact one’s self-esteem, make them angry, and ruin the fun of playing. A way to combat toxicity is to let the person know they are being toxic. Informing them can have two outcomes: the player can apologize and move on or become increasingly offensive. You can also report them for their behavior to the moderation, block, and mute them.
